About Me

Hello! I'm Bwacha! I'm a self-taught Data Science Enthusiast with 2+ years of experience in the Testing Domain. I am highly ambitious and self-motivated, always eager to expand my knowledge of Data Science concepts. I hold a Bachelor's of Engineering degree in Chemical Engineering.

In my learning journey, I prefer working with Python and utilize platforms such as Google Colab and Kaggle. I primarily rely on free and open-source resources like Forage, YouTube, DataCamp, Google's ML programs, FreecodeCamp, Cisco Networking Academy, GitHub, LinkedIn, and extensive online research . I firmly believe in the immense potential of these open-source resources to create wonders.

Aside from my passion for data science, I also enjoy mentoring, writing technical articles and having fun. Currently, I'm a Freelance Technical Writer on Upwork, where I share the social, programming and Machine Learning concepts and insights I have gained. It's my way of giving back to the community that has taught me so much! Additionally, I am an Artist and Hollywood & Anime Lover, pursuing these creative passions alongside my data science endeavors : )

I'm always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow, and I'm excited to see what the future holds for me in data science and Machine Learning!
